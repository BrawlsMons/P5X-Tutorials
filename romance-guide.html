Romance Guide - Persona 5 The Phantom X

Understanding the romance mechanics in P5X and the available romantic options.

How Romance Works in P5X

Romantic partners in Persona 5 The Phantom X are known as Soulmates. You can pursue certain female characters after reaching a high Synergy rank with them, typically at Rank 15. Not all women in the game are dateableâ€”some stay platonic no matter what.

Checking Your Progress

To check your Synergy rank, open the phone menu, choose the Synergy icon, and view your Confidants' details. If someone is romance-eligible, an option to become Soulmates appears at Rank 15; otherwise, you'll only be close friends.

Romance is Optional

Romance isn't required. You can stay friends if you pick the right dialogue choices, and there's no penalty for not dating them. However, you'll earn special profile avatars at Rank 15, with different ones for friendship and romance.

Current Romance Options

Current romance options include Motoha Arai, Tomoko Noge, and Yaoling Li, and more may be introduced in future updates.
