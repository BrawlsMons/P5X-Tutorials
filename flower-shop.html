Flower Shop - Answers

All answers for working at the flower shop.

Question Answer Reward
They want something modest to convey their love. Which flower would you suggest? Red Roses +2 Kindness
They'd like pink flowers to honor an occasion. Pink Carnations +2 Kindness
They'd like large flowers with a mild scent. Golden Gerbera +2 Kindness
They'd like flashy flowers with a rich aroma. Something vibrant and highly fragrant. Sweet Osmanthus +2 Kindness
If a customer asks for bold flowers that convey love, what would you pick out for them? Red Roses +2 Kindness

Flower Shop Tips

  • Working at the flower shop mainly increases Kindness stat
  • Available from the beginning of the game
  • Requires minimum 2 Kindness points to start
  • Pays around 2500-3000 yen per session
